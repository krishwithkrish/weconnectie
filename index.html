How TradeLink Works

A streamlined four-step process that takes your cargo from origin to destination — fully compliant, fully tracked, on time.

01
📝
Submit Your Requirement

Tell us what you're importing — commodity, origin country, volume, and target delivery date. Our team will prepare a tailored freight quote within 4 hours.

02
🗂️
Documentation & Compliance

We prepare all required import documents — commercial invoices, packing lists, bills of lading, certificates of origin — and handle customs entry filings in the destination country.

03
📦

Your cargo is booked, consolidated if needed, and dispatched via ocean or air. We manage customs clearance at the port of entry — duties assessed and paid on your behalf.

04
🚀
Last-Mile Delivery

From the port to your warehouse or customer's door. Live tracking updates at every milestone — you always know exactly where your goods are.

02

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of goods can TradeLink handle? ⌄

We handle a wide range of commodities including industrial machinery, consumer electronics, textiles and apparel, food and agricultural products, chemicals (non-hazardous), construction materials, and retail merchandise. Dangerous goods and specialized cargo can be arranged with prior notice.

How do you ensure customs compliance? ⌄

Our in-house licensed customs brokers have expertise in over 60 trade jurisdictions. We classify all goods using the Harmonized System (HS) code, calculate applicable duties and taxes, and prepare fully compliant declarations — reducing the risk of delays, fines, or seizures at the border.

How long does ocean freight typically take? ⌄

Transit times depend on the trade lane. Asia to Europe typically takes 25–32 days; Asia to the Middle East 10–18 days; Asia to North America 14–22 days. Air freight cuts these times to 2–7 days for most routes. We provide estimated arrival dates with every booking confirmation.

Which countries and ports do you serve? ⌄

We operate across 80+ countries with direct services into 200+ major seaports and 150+ airports. Key hubs include Singapore, Dubai, Hamburg, Rotterdam, Los Angeles, Shanghai, Mumbai, and Colombo. We also offer inland haulage solutions in most markets.

Do you offer cargo insurance? ⌄

Yes. We offer all-risk cargo insurance covering physical loss or damage during transit. Coverage is available on a per-shipment or open-cover (annual) basis. Our insurance partners include globally rated underwriters, and claims are processed within 30 business days.

How do I get a freight quote? ⌄

Simply fill in the contact form on this page or call us directly. Provide the origin and destination, commodity type, approximate weight and dimensions, and your preferred shipping mode. We respond with a detailed quote within 4 business hours on all standard trade lanes.
